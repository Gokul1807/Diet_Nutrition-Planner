Sustainable Development Goals & Diet Planner

What are the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)?

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) are a set of 17 global objectives set by the United Nations to ensure a better and more sustainable future for all.

How Does Our Diet & Nutrition Planner Align with SDG 3?

My application supports SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being by:

  • Providing personalized meal plans based on dietary needs and health goals.
  • Helping users make healthier food choices to prevent lifestyle diseases.
  • Encouraging balanced nutrition and fitness for overall well-being.
